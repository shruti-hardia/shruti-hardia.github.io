About Me

I am a Senior Full Stack .NET Developer and Cloud Engineer with over ten years of experience building scalable, secure, and high-performance enterprise applications and cloud-native platforms. My expertise spans modern .NET technologies, distributed systems engineering, and cloud infrastructure design, enabling organizations to deliver reliable digital solutions at scale.

By combining deep expertise in ASP.NET Core, microservices architecture, and cloud-native engineering with strong research foundations, I design robust, fault-tolerant, and secure systems for high-compliance industries such as healthcare, cybersecurity, and enterprise platforms.
